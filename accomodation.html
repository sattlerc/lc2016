Leeds has a wide variety of bridges. Many smaller ones can be found along the canal connecting Leeds to Liverpool. Be sure to book in time as each typically accomodates just a few people underneath.

If everything else is already booked out, you might resort to one of the Ring Road crossings. Previous lodgers commonly complained about above-average noise levels disturbing their sleep. However, added value is found in proximity to the university campus.
